The most beautiful beaches in Vietnam

The most beautiful beaches in Vietnam

#1. PHU QUOC BEACHES

Phu Quoc is the largest tourist island in Vietnam, Long Beach (Phu Quoc) "A paradise with golden sunshine, cool water and unspoiled quiet space", located in the northwest of the island. Phu Quoc (Kien Giang) is 15km long and is likened to a paradise with golden sunshine, cool water and a quiet and unspoiled space. This place is also included in the list of the most beautiful and clean beaches in the world by ABC News (Australia) but still few people know about it. Besides, CNN travel experts also assessed that Phu Quoc is making great strides to rise to its position as one of the most famous islands in Vietnam.

#2. MY KHE BEACH

My Khe Beach was once recognized by Forbes magazine as one of the six most beautiful beaches in the Central region of our country. With a length of 900m, My Khe beach appears with clear water, smooth sand stretches and rows of coconut trees swaying in the wind. If you want to find a peaceful place during your trip to the central region, this beach is the ideal choice. My Khe beach is calm, the water is clear and warm, so it is very safe for both adults and children. Coming here, in addition to swimming, visitors can also enjoy seafood or enjoy services from a system of bars, restaurants, and hotels around.

#3. QUY NHON BEACH

If you have the opportunity to come to Quy Nhon city, which is full of sunshine and typical wind, you must definitely go to Quy Nhon beach to enjoy the beauty of the long coastline of iridescent golden sand embracing the blue waves of the sea. . Zooming out a little further, you will see a fresh ocean blue mixed with the color of the sky as clear as jade. The beaches in Quy Nhon have the intact beauty of nature. Eo Gio and Ky Co are some of the most famous beaches here.

#4. LANG CO BEACH

Lang Co Bay impresses visitors at first sight with its poetic and sweet beauty that is hard to describe in words. Those are white sand dunes, clear sea water, and immense mountains. This is also the reason why Lang Co Bay often appears in Hue tours. The view from Hai Van Pass looking down to Lang Co Bay is the widest and most comprehensive view. It seems that the whole Lang Co is in sight, indescribably peaceful. The sea water of Lang Co Bay is famous for its clear blue, long white sand beach, the average temperature is only about 25 degrees Celsius, the air is extremely cool and fresh, it is a wonderful resort destination for tourists. guest. Not only that, but Lang Co also attracts visitors to come here to learn and admire the majestic mountain scenery, blending into the immense sea and the immense green of the tropical primeval forest.

#5. HA LONG BEACH

Ha Long Bay is recognized by Unesco as the World's Natural Heritage with thousands of islands created by a magnificent and lively creation. The bay has beautiful scenery, so it is a very attractive tourist destination for domestic and international tourists. Ha Long Bay is a great work of visual art of nature with the presence of thousands of rocky islands of all shapes and sizes, with many interesting caves gathering into a world that is both vivid and mysterious. Famous landscapes such as Cat Ba Island, Tuan Chau Island, Bai Tu Long Bay will surely bring you great experiences. Therefore, if you are wondering between tourist destinations, Ha Long Bay is a great suggestion. Ha Long Bay is a work of art made by the hands of nature with the presence of thousands of rocky islands of all shapes and sizes, many interesting caves gathering into a world that is both vivid and mysterious. recognized by Unesco as a World Natural Heritage. Famous landscapes such as Cat Ba Island, Tuan Chau Island, Bai Tu Long Bay will surely bring you great experiences. Therefore, Ha Long Bay is a destination not to be missed when traveling in Vietnam.
